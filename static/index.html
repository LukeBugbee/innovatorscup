8VC Social
Innovator’s Cup

Entrepreneurs that tap into the energy and compassion that animate American communities can solve major social challenges. That’s why we’re deploying over $1 million in the coming years to innovative student projects that lift up the world and serve those in need.

The 8VC Social Innovator’s Cup is designed to encourage the best and brightest North American college students to create original projects that serve those in need.

Our competition will highlight high-impact contrarian projects inspired by a sense of civic duty and oriented towards underprivileged communities.

Prizes and Benefits

Cash prizes
up to $88,000

We want to accelerate your organization through funding. For 2018, cash prizes totaling $136K will be rewarded with $88,000 for first place and five $8,000 prizes.

Promotion, Exposure, and Mentorship

Participants will receive mentorship from top leaders who understand how to scale philanthropic impact because they’ve done it themselves. These experts are excited to work with high-energy students with impactful ideas.

Access to Leaders and Policy Makers

Many business leaders and policy-makers in our network are excited to work with the finalists and winners of the 8VC Social Innovator’s Cup.

Contest Guidelines

School leaderboard

8VC will award a bonus $8,000 prize to teams from the University with the most applications. The bonus prize will be equally split between those teams.

Share with your peers and may the best school win!
